Welcome to the CNICS Data Query Tool

CNICS is comprised of 10 HIV clinics at academic medical centers. Those sites combine detailed and anonymized data reflecting context, treatment and outcomes in the day-to-day care of people with HIV, through National Institute of Allergy and Infectious Diseases (NIAID). CNICS supports translational, clinical, and behavioral HIV research, and welcomes proposals from any researcher.

This data query tool helps us implement NIH principles of making research data Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable. We use the Leaf framework to allow high level exploration of CNICS data.
